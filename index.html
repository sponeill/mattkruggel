Matt Kruggel

Matt Kruggel is originally from Sharon Center, Ohio and holds degrees in Musical Theatre and Spanish from Ashland University. Shortly after graduating, he spent some time in the Chicago theatre world performing at such theatres as Chicago Shakespeare Theatre, Theatre at the Center, Quest Theatre Ensemble, Theatre Building Chicago and Porchlight Music Theatre. Favorite past roles include: Matt in FANTASTICKS, Rapunzel's Prince in INTO THE WOODS, Jimmy in THOROUGHLY MODERN MILLIE and Benjy Stone in MY FAVORITE YEAR. New York Credit: Howard Carter in THE MYSTERY OF KING TUT (Theatreworks/USA)
